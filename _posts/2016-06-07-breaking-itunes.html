--- layout: post title: Breaking iTunes date: '2016-06-07T22:30:00.001-04:00' author: George Peckham-Rooney tags: - Technology -- Apple modified_time: '2016-07-02T15:19:22.347-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3562148025286811645.post-1028076643129088346 blogger_orig_url: https://georgepeckhamrooney.blogspot.com/2016/06/breaking-itunes.html --- I came across this great article on 9to5mac today on how the podcasts function should be removed from iTunes and made into a stand alone application. I have often thought how ITunes needs to change as it is trying to do so many things for so many people. I have advocated for seperating the media playing section from the more device management section though I thought the article made a succient case for breaking apart the different media functions into seperate apps (it would mirror the iOS at that point too, which seems to be Apples general plan.) I highly recommend you check it and would love to hear your thoughts on the matter.
